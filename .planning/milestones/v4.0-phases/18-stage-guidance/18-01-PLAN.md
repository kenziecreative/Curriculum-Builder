Purpose: SMEs currently see gate decisions with no evaluation guidance — they approve or reject based on gut feel. Self-check questions give them a sharp-colleague framework for evaluation. Bloom labels like "recall through create" mean nothing to non-ID professionals.

Output: Updated approve.md with self-check questions at both gates + natural thinking-level language; updated assessments.md with self-check questions at the PIPE-05 gate.
</objective>
