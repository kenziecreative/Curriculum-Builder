## Essential Questions

1. If your business is profitable, why might you still be unable to make payroll?
2. What does your cash flow statement tell you that your bank balance doesn't?
3. When you're facing a cash gap, how do you decide which lever to pull — and how do you know if you're making the right call?
4. What's the difference between a cash problem you can solve in 30 days and one that's already too late to fix?
5. What would it mean to run your business from your cash flow statement instead of your bank balance?
