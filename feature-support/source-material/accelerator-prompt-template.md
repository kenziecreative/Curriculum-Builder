## The Task

Your task is to design a comprehensive 12-week accelerator for busy entrepreneurs. This accelerator should deliver transformative learning experiences while accommodating the time constraints of the participants.

Your design should include:

The curriculum for 12-weeks

The accompanying worksheets or guides

Associated email or SMS messages

One external expert session in each month

Throughout this process, ensure your plan:

Engages the target audience of busy professionals

Delivers transformative learning experiences

Balances comprehensive content with time efficiency

Accommodates various learning styles

Aligns with the "4E" approach (Engage, Explore, Experiment, Embed)

Follow these steps to create your accelerator plan:

### Step 1: Define Accelerator Objective and Craft Promise

1.1 Review and Align

Review the accelerator type specified in the <accelerator-type> tag

Familiarize yourself with the 'Key Features' section of the chosen accelerator type in the 'Accelerator Types' document

1.2 Define Accelerator Objective

Create a clear and tangible objective and benefits statement that:

Aligns with the chosen accelerator type

Focuses on one of the following outcomes:

Teaching attendees how to achieve a specific goal or complete a process

Providing a clear answer to a question or aiding in understanding a specific concept

Enhancing a skill or expanding the attendees' toolkit

Addressing a common concern or teaching how to make a meaningful change

1.3 Craft Accelerator Promise

Develop a concise and specific promise that:

Clearly communicates the accelerator's value

Incorporates at least two key features from the chosen accelerator type

Is detailed enough for potential attendees to understand its relevance to their needs

1.4 Refine and Present

Brainstorm 5 potential variations of the promise, each slightly emphasizing different aspects of the accelerator's value

Ensure each variation aligns with the defined objective

Present the objective and the 5 promise variations to the user for feedback and selection

1.5 Finalize

Based on user feedback, finalize the accelerator objective and selected promise

Ensure strong alignment between the objective and the chosen promise
